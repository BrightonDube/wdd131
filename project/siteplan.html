RecipeMaster

Your Personal Recipe Collection & Meal Planning Solution

Site Purpose

RecipeMaster is a comprehensive digital platform designed for home cooks and meal planning enthusiasts. The site serves as a centralized hub where users can discover, save, and organize their favorite recipes while creating personalized meal plans. Our goal is to simplify meal planning and cooking by providing intuitive tools and a beautiful, user-friendly interface.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Busy Parent

"I need to plan healthy meals for my family for the upcoming week. I want to find quick, nutritious recipes that my picky kids will actually eat, and then generate a shopping list based on my meal plan."

Scenario 2: Cooking Enthusiast

"I'm looking for new dinner ideas that fit my dietary restrictions (vegetarian, gluten-free). I'd love to save recipes I find online and organize them into my own digital cookbook with custom categories."

Color Usage:

  • Forest Green (#1B5E20): Headers, primary buttons, and key navigation elements (AAA contrast with white text)
  • Brick Red (#C62828): Secondary buttons, highlights, and important call-to-actions
  • Dark Orange (#E65100): Accents, badges, and special highlights
  • Dark Gray (#212121): Main text color with excellent contrast on light backgrounds
  • Light Gray (#f5f5f5): Background and card colors
