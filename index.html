About Me

I'm Brighton Dube, originally from Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, now living in Sandton, Johannesburg. As a restaurant manager, I've spent decades mentoring young professionals. My greatest joys are my family, weekend golf games, and my rose garden. I'm now exploring web development as my next adventure.

Sandton, Johannesburg

Sandton is Johannesburg's bustling financial hub, known as "Africa's richest square mile." Beyond its skyscrapers, you'll find the Nelson Mandela Square, beautiful parks, and vibrant arts at the Sandton Convention Centre. What I love most is how green the city is - from the rooftop gardens to the tree-lined streets of my neighborhood.
